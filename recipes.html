Quinoa & Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook quinoa and let cool.
  2. Mix with avocado and tomatoes.
  3. Drizzle with lemon juice, season, and serve.

Berry Protein Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 cup almond milk

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled.

Grilled Chicken & Veggies

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with salt & pepper.
  2. Grill until cooked through.
  3. Roast broccoli and carrots with olive oil.
  4. Serve together.

Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Steps:

  1. Mix oats, milk, and chia seeds in jar.
  2. Refrigerate overnight.
  3. Top with banana before serving.

Salmon Power Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Sesame dressing

Steps:

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. Grill salmon until flaky.
  3. Assemble bowl with rice, spinach, and salmon.
  4. Drizzle with sesame dressing.

Chicken & Hummus Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 wholegrain wrap
  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • Fresh greens

Steps:

  1. Spread hummus on wrap.
  2. Add chicken and greens.
  3. Roll tightly and slice in half.
