Where We Are

Welcome to MK Fitness, your premier health and fitness centre located in Leadenhall, Milton Keynes.

We pride ourselves on offering more than just a gym — we’re a community dedicated to helping you achieve your goals.

Our facilities include a spacious gym with state-of-the-art equipment, a swimming pool, and a relaxing sauna. For sports lovers, we offer tennis and squash courts, plus a large indoor hall for football, badminton, and more.

Whether you're here to build strength, improve fitness, or simply enjoy an active lifestyle, MK Fitness has everything you need. Join us today and discover why we're Milton Keynes' home for health, fitness, and community.
